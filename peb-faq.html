    FAQ about PEB

    Home Pre Engineered Building FAQ about PEB

    Yes, Pre-Engineered Buildings are designed to withstand diverse weather conditions, including snow, high desert temperatures, high humidity, heavy rainfall, cyclone-prone areas, and seismic zones. Each building is customized based on site-specific ambient conditions and intended usage.

    Yes, we offer comprehensive steel building packages, including standard accessories like mezzanine floors, roll-up doors, and cranes. Steel framing and decking are provided for floors, and other flooring options like pre-cast concrete can be accommodated as needed.

    There are no size limitations. Techno Fab can handle projects ranging from small sheds to massive industrial complexes spanning thousands of square meters.

    PEBs require fewer column foundations due to larger spans and lighter dead loads compared to conventional construction. This results in significant savings in foundation costs, though exact savings depend on the project type.

    We can design PEBs using either local codes (e.g., Indian Standards) or international standards. While international codes offer optimized safety and cost-efficiency, we comply with client preferences for local standards as well.

    You can send your inquiry to sales@technofabglobal.com with details such as project size, site conditions, and usage requirements. We will provide a customized proposal, including scope, pricing, and delivery timelines.

    Yes, we provide detailed Anchor Bolt plans, column reactions, and base plate designs to guide the foundation construction according to site-specific conditions.

    All field connections are bolted, eliminating the need for on-site welding. This ensures faster and more precise assembly of the building.

    Yes, PEBs are designed for flexibility. They can be dismantled, transported, and reassembled at new locations. Costs for relocation depend on the building's size, complexity, and distance.

    Yes, masonry walls can be tied to steel columns or isolated for structural optimization. We also provide designs for beams to support masonry walls above openings if needed.

    PEBs offer several advantages, including:
    • Faster construction times.
    • Lower material costs due to optimized design.
    • Reduced foundation requirements.
    • High flexibility for future modifications or expansions.
    • Superior durability with minimal maintenance needs.

    PEBs are widely used in:
    • Industrial warehouses and factories.
    • Commercial complexes and shopping malls.
    • Aircraft hangars and workshops.
    • Schools, auditoriums, and community centers.
    • Agricultural storage units, cold storage, and dairy farms.
    • Residential car sheds and small utility spaces.

    Yes, PEBs are highly cost-effective due to:
    • Reduced construction time and labor.
    • Optimized material usage with minimal waste.
    • Long-term savings through lower maintenance costs.
    • Energy-efficient designs with options for insulation and ventilation.

    The completion time depends on the size and complexity of the project. Typically, PEBs take 6–12 weeks for design, manufacturing, and delivery after finalizing the specifications. Erection time is significantly faster than conventional construction.

    Yes, concrete floors can be installed on steel decks provided over steel framing. The steel deck acts as a shuttering system for casting reinforced concrete slabs.

    Temperature control can be achieved with roof and wall insulation. For advanced temperature regulation, HVAC systems can be integrated into the building design.

    Fire resistance is achieved by applying fire-resistant coatings to steel members or enclosing them with approved materials like gypsum boards. We design fire protection systems based on specific fire-rating requirements.

    Yes, we provide detailed design calculations, erection drawings, and on-site technical support to assist with assembly.

    Yes, PEBs can be designed with provisions for future expansions, such as additional loading or increased dimensions. On-site modifications for unforeseen requirements can also be carried out.

    Yes, we offer portable units that are pre-assembled or designed for easy dismantling and transportation. These are ideal for field offices, workshops, or storage facilities.
