    Technofab Industrial Fabrication

    Welcome to Techno Fab, a leader in heavy industrial steel fabrication and engineering solutions. We specialize in the fabrication, machining, and assembly of steel structures, fuel handling systems, and industrial process equipment, serving a wide range of industries. Our expertise covers heavy structural fabrication, custom fuel handling equipment, and special-purpose products for the boiler, process, and sugar sectors. We also provide construction equipment, foundation bolts, gratings, and hand railings, ensuring innovative, high-quality, and reliable solutions tailored to meet your project requirements.

    Technofab Steel Buildings

    Techno Fab offers efficient building solutions focused on speed, sustainability, and flexibility. Our Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEBs) provide durable, custom-designed steel structures for warehouses and factories with fast construction. Sustainable Steel Buildings feature energy-efficient designs, recycled materials, and reduced waste for eco-friendly construction. We also provide customizable, portable office containers designed for on-site workspace needs, ensuring cost-effective and reliable solutions.
