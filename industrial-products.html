    Industrial Products

    Techno Fab Industrial Fabrication Products Welcome to Techno Fab, your trusted partner for reliable industrial fabrication services. We are dedicated to delivering excellence and precision in every project, ensuring your requirements are met with the highest standards.

    A proud sister concern of Comfort Solutions, established in 2016, Techno Fab specializes in heavy industrial fabrication for diverse industries. Backed by years of expertise and a team of skilled professionals, we focus on quality craftsmanship and timely execution. Our modern facilities and efficient processes enable us to tackle even the most complex fabrication tasks with ease and accuracy.

    Our Services to meet the diverse needs of our clients

    1. Custom Fabrication

    Staircase and Hand Railings, Ball Type Hand Railings, Gratings and Grating Clamps, Expansion Bellows, Cable Trays, Stainless Steel Bubble Caps, Stainless Steel Ferrules, Foundation Bolts, Pressure Part Support, Dampers with and without Actuators, Sliding Gates, Airfoils, Oil Cooler, Bed Plate / Air Distribution Plate, APH Tube Sheets, Air Nozzles, Ash Drain Gates, Base Frames

    2. Engineering and Design

    CAD Design Services: Advanced computer-aided design for precision and accuracy.

    Prototyping: Creating prototypes to ensure the design meets your requirements before full-scale production.

    3. Welding and Assembly

    Specialized Welding: Expertise in MIG, TIG, and arc welding for strong and durable joins.

    Component Assembly: Assembling fabricated parts into final products or systems.

    4. Maintenance and Repair

    Industrial Equipment Repair: Keeping your equipment in top condition with our repair services.

    Maintenance Services: Regular maintenance to prevent downtime and extend the lifespan of your machinery.

    Fabricated Product Range

  • Structural Steel Fabrication: Columns, Beams, Bracings, Girders, Base Frames, Racks, Trusses, Bolted Structures
  • Equipment: Tanks (SS and MS), Process Equipment, Storage Tanks, ESP Ducting
  • Surface Preparation and Painting: Grit Blasting, HB Zinc Phosphate, HB Zinc Silicate, Alkyd Zinc Phosphate, Epoxy Zinc Phosphate, Zinc Silicate, Synthetic Enamel, Polyurethane, and Fire Protection Paints tailored to clients' specifications.
  • Industrial Fabricated Components: Ducting (Round and Square), Insulated Ducting, Boiler Casing, Hopper, Bag Filter for ESP, Fan Casing, Chimney, Piping, Chimney Supporting Structure, Boiler Supporting Structure, Foundation Bolts, Packers Plates, Pressure Part Supports, Shop Attachments, Fabricated Silencer, Economizer Casings, Wind Box Assembly, Bunker, Silo, Mechanical Dust Collector, Bed Plate for Boiler Assembly, Fabricated Dampers, SS / MS Piping and Pipe Supports, Stainless Steel Fabrications, Fuel Feeding Chute, Bed Plate for Boiler Assembly
  • Products We Offer: Pre Engineered Buildings (PEB), Foundation Bolts and Sag Rods, Pressure Part Support, Bed Plates Assembly, Fabricated Dampers and Silencers, Ball Type Railings, Standard and Layout Type Industrial Hand Rails, Staircase, Grating, and Railings Assembly, Metallic Expansion Bellows, Air Flow Measuring Device, Alarm System, Peep Hole Assembly, Oil Cooler
