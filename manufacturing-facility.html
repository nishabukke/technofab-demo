    Collaborative Excellence in Pre-Engineered Buildings

    At Techno Fab, we believe in transparency and delivering the best for our clients. While we do not currently have a fully integrated facility for Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) manufacturing, we collaborate with trusted manufacturing partners who operate world-class facilities equipped with advanced machinery and technologies.

    These strategic partnerships enable us to leverage cutting-edge capabilities and maintain high-quality production standards, ensuring that every PEB solution meets your unique requirements. By combining our project management expertise with the resources of our network, we deliver precision, efficiency, and excellence in every project.

    This collaborative approach reflects our commitment to honesty, quality, and customer satisfaction, ensuring you receive unparalleled solutions without compromise.

    These facilities have a range of specialized machines needed to create high-quality PEB components:

  • CNC Plasma Cutting Machine: CNC Plasma cut steel plates accurately, ensuring precise dimensions for strong and reliable structures.
  • Pull-Through Submerged Arc Welding Line: These welding lines make strong H-beams and other key parts with consistent quality for large projects.
  • Machining Facility: This facility processes foundation bolts and sag rods, ensuring they are precise and durable for secure connections.
  • CNC Roll Forming Machine: Roll Forming machines create purlins, claddings, and roofing sheets with accuracy and consistency, supporting different designs.
  • Deck Sheet Roll Forming Machines: These machines produce strong deck sheets with precise dimensions, perfect for load-bearing applications.
  • Shot Blasting Machine and Booth: These machines clean and prepare steel surfaces for better paint adhesion and longer durability.
  • Painting Booths: Modern painting booths apply even and durable coatings that meet high-quality standards.
  • Shearing Machines: These machines cut steel plates and sheets precisely, ensuring clean and accurate results.
  • Press Brakes: Used for bending and shaping steel, these machines help create custom designs for PEB accessories.
  • Specialized Press Brakes for Polycarbonate Sheets and Louvers: These machines create polycarbonate sheets and louvers with precision, adding both functionality and style.
  • Overhead Cranes: Overhead cranes handle heavy parts safely and efficiently during assembly and erection.

    • With the help of these advanced facilities and expert partnerships, Techno Fab ensures that every PEB project is built with quality, precision, and care. Our commitment to honesty, innovation, and customer satisfaction drives us to deliver solutions that exceed expectations. With Techno Fab, you get reliable and top-class PEB solutions for your needs.
